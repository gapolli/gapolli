### 🚀 Main Project: Pluggable Modular Blockchain
I am currently developing a **native, non-EVM Proof of Authority (PoA) blockchain** featuring a multi-language **WASM smart contract runtime**. 
* **Core Engine:** Built in Python and C++, with plans to be ported to **Golang** in the future for high performance.
* **Environment:** The ecosystem currently runs locally and inside **isolated containers** for testing and orchestration.
* **Core & Infrastructure Modules:** Fully equipped with essential network layers, including **Governance**, **Indexer**, **Wallet**, **DA (Data Availability)**, **Bridge**, **IAM (Identity and Access Management)**, and **Dash (Dashboard)**.
* **Creative Modules:** Designed with highly pluggable layers natively integrated, connecting software engineering with creative concepts:
  * 🎵 **Music** & 🎨 **Paint** modules.
  * 🤖 **Agents**, 📡 **Beam**, and 🧬 **Gene** modules.
  * *More experimental layers are actively under development.*

### 🛠️ Other Side Projects
